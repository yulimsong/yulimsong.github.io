A Manual of OGift

Process to make the work

  1. Request data by e-mail.
    • A name, alias, initials, etc. that can refer to you.
    • Address and contact information to receive the gift.
  2. Create gifts based on the received data.
    • Making embroidery or knitting work in the form of a fabric: embroidering/knitting the name(initial or nickname) or text, etc.
  3. Sending the gift by post.
